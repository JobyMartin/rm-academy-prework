Homemade Pizza

Description

This is a classic homemade pizza with soft dough, rich tomato sauce, and melted cheese baked until golden and crispy.

Ingredients:

Pizza Dough:

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast, 1 packet
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Toppings:

  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Cooked sausage
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Extra Parmesan cheese

Steps:

Prep Work

  1. Measure out all ingredients before starting.
  2. Preheat oven to 475°.

Make the Dough

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, olive oil, and salt.
  3. Mix until dough forms, then knead for about 8–10 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.

Make the Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, combine marinara sauce, olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and salt.
  2. Stir until well mixed and set aside.

Shape the Pizza

  1. Punch down the dough and place on a floured surface.
  2. Roll or stretch the dough into a circle.
  3. Place dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet.

Assemble

  1. Spread sauce evenly over the dough.
  2. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  3. Add desired toppings.
  4. Finish with a little extra Parmesan cheese.

Bake

  1. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  2. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes.
  3. Slice and serve.
