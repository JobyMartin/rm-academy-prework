Lasagna

Description

This is a classic italian dish consisting of layers of flat noodles, sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients:

Cheese Filling:

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • ¾ lb. ground beef
  • ¾ lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna Noodles/Cheese Topping:

  • 12 lasagna noodles, plus extra in case of breakage
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps:

Prep Work

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients in a bowl; set aside. Measure remaining ingredients.

Make the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions; cook until softened (about 10 minutes).
  2. Add ground beef and sausage; increase to medium-high. Break into fine crumbles and cook 8–10 minutes until done. Add garlic in the last minute. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add chicken broth, scraping the pot. Stir in marinara, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and simmer while cooking noodles.

Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Boil salted water. Cook noodles to al dente per package instructions, stirring occasionally. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Lay noodles flat on wax or parchment paper.

Assemble

  1. Spread 1 cup meat sauce in a 9×13 dish. Add 4 noodles.
  2. Spread 1/3 ricotta mixture, then 1½ cups meat sauce.
  3. Add 4 noodles, 1/3 ricotta, and 1½ cups meat sauce.
  4. Add final 4 noodles, remaining ricotta, and remaining meat sauce.
  5. Top with 2½ cups mozzarella.
  6. Cover with sprayed foil (spray side down).

Bake

  1. Bake 35 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes.
  2. Garnish with parsley. Let rest 15 minutes before serving. Serve with garlic bread.
