Fettuccine Alfredo

Home

Description

This is a rich, creamy Italian pasta dish consisting of fettuccine noodles tossed in a smooth butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese sauce.

Ingredients:

Alfredo Sauce:

  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ cup mozzarella cheese, optional

Pasta:

  • 12 oz fettuccine noodles
  • Water for boiling
  • 1 tablespoon salt, for pasta water

Optional Toppings:

  • Grilled chicken, sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Extra Parmesan cheese

Steps:

Prep Work

  1. Grate the Parmesan cheese, mince the garlic, and measure out all ingredients before starting.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the pasta.

Cook the Pasta

  1. Add 1 tablespoon salt to the boiling water.
  2. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Stir occasionally so the noodles don’t stick.
  4. Drain the pasta, but do not rinse. Set aside.

Make the Alfredo Sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant but not browned.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well.
  4. Bring to a gentle simmer, then reduce heat to low.
  5. Add the Parmesan cheese slowly while stirring continuously.
  6. Add salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  7. Stir until the sauce becomes smooth and thick.
  8. Add mozzarella cheese if using, and stir until melted.

Combine

  1. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce.
  2. Use tongs to toss the noodles until fully coated in the Alfredo sauce.
  3. Let it cook for 1–2 minutes so the sauce sticks to the pasta.

Serve

  1. Remove from heat and top with extra Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley.
  2. Add grilled chicken if desired.
  3. Serve immediately while hot.
